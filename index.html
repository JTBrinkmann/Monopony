History

This game is developed and maintained by Brinkie Pie.
It all started with a gamejam. TAT and Colgate planned on doing a livestream showing them coding an online game called "Monopony", based on the custom Monopoly board by sup3rgh0st. They initially did some progress (displaying the board, having a zoom-in/-out functionality and data of the positions of the fields) but after a few hours the connection to Colgate was lost. TAT continued for a few hours alone, before aborting the project until he could reach Colgate again… sadly they never continued. This is were I - Brinkie Pie - kicked in. I continued the project on my own but quickly realised, that this game needs more than a few hours of coding. Thus, many days were spend and the little TAT and Colgate did was completely rewritten and improved. The only thing that remained was the position of the fields.

As I noticed how much work this got, I looked out for help by friends. Thankfully, Canpan offered his help. He added the raw data for the cards and fields (what is a utility field, what isn't, mortages, house costs, rent, etc) which I then could implement. He also bug-tested the game and helped me fix many problems.
And now, the game is what you can see here! Well most likely THIS isn't the most up to date version, but good enough
